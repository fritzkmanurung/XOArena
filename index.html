How to Play

Tic Tac Toe is a game where two players take turns marking spaces in a 3×3 grid.

X

Player X goes first

O

Player O goes second

The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) wins

If all 9 squares are full and there's no winner, the game ends in a draw

Game Over
